Feed-forward loops are of two types: Coherent feed-forward loop and Incoherent feed-forward loop. Both coherent feed-forward loop and incoherent feed-forward loopare composed of three genes X, Y, and Z where X regulates Y and Z and Y regulates only Z and the interaction is mediated by two signals SX and SY. These signals can act as an inhibitor molecule or as an activator molecule that binds X, Y and Z. The regulation of X on Y, X on Z or Y on Z can be either activation or Inhibition and signals SX and SY can mediate the regulation with either presence (1) or absence (0). Complex gene network can be modeled by using the coherent feed-forward loop and incoherent feed-forward loop, termed as Interlocked feed-forward loop.
